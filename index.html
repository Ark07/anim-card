Tiger

Every tiger in the world is unique - no two tigers have the same pattern of stripes.

  • Scientific Name : Panthera tigris
  • Lifespan: 20-26 years
  • Height: 70-120 centimeters
  • Speed: 49-65 km/hr

The tiger (Panthera tigris) is the largest cat species, most recognisable for their pattern of dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur with a lighter underside. The largest wild tiger ever reported had a total body length of 3.38 m (11.1 ft) over curves and weighed 388.7 kg (857 lb).
